## OpenMythos

* <https://github.com/kyegomez/OpenMythos>

- "theoretical" implementation of the Claude Mythos  model.
- It implements a Recurrent-Depth Transformer (RDT) with three stages: 

  - Prelude (transformer blocks), 
  - a looped Recurrent Block (up to max_loop_iters), 
  - and a final Coda.

 Attention is switchable between MLA and GQA, and the feed-forward uses
a sparse MoE with routed and shared experts ideal for exploring 
compute-adaptive, depth-variable reasoning.
