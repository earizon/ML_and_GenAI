## Planning and decision making using (only) LLMs

Smart planning and decision making are critical to achieving predefined goals.

LLMs, trained on vast amount of world knowledge and human examples, 
are known to have already absorbed rich commonsense that makes it possible
to propose reasonable plans conditioned on problem setting and environmental states 
(Huang et al., 2022a; Zhang et al., 2023; Wang et al., 2023b;
 Huang et al., 2022b; Wang et al., 2023a; Yao et al., 2022; Yang et al., 2023). 

WARN:
- It does not means that LLMs are the best tools to create plans,
  it just means that "somehow" it is possible to use LLMs.<br/>
  Other tools like algorithm or optimization planners could be
  a better or more efficient solution.
