# Google ADK Agent Development Kit

* OpenSource framework to build AI Agents and Agents Swarms.
* Clean and simple architecture and posibility to use 
  different stacks (Python, Java, Golang).
* simple agent definitions through YAML [[PM.low_code]]
* Multiple deployment scenarios (container, k8s, Google Cloud, ...)
* Batteries included from Google Gemmini and Google Vertex.
  Other LLMs, Vector DDBBS, ... can be added by just implementing
  the requiredrequired  interfaces.
