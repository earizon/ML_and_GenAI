## Agent2Agent (A2A) protocol

* <https://github.com/google/A2A>

One of the biggest challenges in enterprise AI adoption is getting agents built 
on different frameworks and vendors to work together. That’s why we created 
an open Agent2Agent (A2A) protocol, a collaborative way to help agents across 
different ecosystems communicate with each other.

- With A2A, agents can show each other their capabilities and negotiate how 
they will interact with users (via text, forms, or bidirectional audio/video).
all while working securely together.
