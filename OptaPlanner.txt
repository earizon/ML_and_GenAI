# OptaPlanner [[{qa.enterprise_patterns.optimization]]
* AI constraint solver to optimize planning and scheduling problems,
  such as the Vehicle Routing Problem, Employee Rostering,
  Maintenance Scheduling, Task Assignment, School Timetabling,
  Cloud Optimization, Conference Scheduling, Job Shop Scheduling,
  Bin Packing and many more.
* It "compares" with OR-Tools, Multi Echelon and others.
* Every organization faces such challenges:
  1. assign a limited set of constrained resources (employees,
     assets, time and/or money) to provide products or services.
  OptaPlanner delivers more efficient plans, which reduce costs and
  improve service quality.  [[}]]
