[[{mlops.ray]]
## Ray: AI compute engine.

  Ray consists of a core distributed runtime and a set of AI Libraries for 
accelerating ML workloads.

* <https://github.com/ray-project/ray>

Different Ray AI Libraries allows for:

- Data: Scalable Datasets for ML.           [[scalability.dataset]]
- Train: Distributed Training.              [[scalability.distributed,training.distributed]]
- Tune: Scalable Hyperparameter Tuning.     [[training.tunning]]
- RLlib: Scalable Reinforcement Learning.   [[training.RL]]
- Serve: Scalable and Programmable Serving.
