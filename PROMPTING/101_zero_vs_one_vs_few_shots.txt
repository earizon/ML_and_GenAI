## Basic prompting: Zero-shot vs One-shot vs Few-shots

- Basic prompting does bare use the ability of the LLM for reasoning. 
- It enhances over standard search engines by providing focuses answers without even
  having the complete information to pose the correct question. The LLM can resolve
  the "Unkown unknowns". (The question you don't ask to a search engine because you
  are not aware of them in first place).
