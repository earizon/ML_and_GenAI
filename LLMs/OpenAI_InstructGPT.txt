## InstructGPT (OpenAI, 2022): LLM to Follow Human Instructions 

* <https://www.infoq.com/news/2022/02/openai-instructgpt/>


 InstructGPT (GPT-3) address complaints about toxic language and misinformation.

... To make the models safer, helpful, and aligned to follow instrunctions, OpenAI used
reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) to fine-tune GPT-3.

- Compared to standard GPT-3, the new InstructGPT models are better at following 
  instructions in English, less inclined to produce misinformation, and at least
  slightly less likely to produce toxic results. 

 OpenAI designed a three-step procedure.

1. fine-tuning of the model.
2. building a reward model (RM).
3. take the Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) model and further fine-tune it using reinforcement learning.

GPT-3 surpasses InstructGPT in terms NLP benchmarks, but it’s better adapted to human intention.

 The AI alignment problem is a well-known problem in the field. It defines the difficulty of designing
AI systems that understand our values, beliefs, and desires, and that behave in a way that won't
interfere with them.
