## Autoregressive Transformer Language Model (ATLM)

ATLM is the most generic term to refer to an LLM model based on the Transformer 
architecture and trained to predict the next token.

- Autoregressive: The model generates text by predicting one token at a time, 
   conditioning each prediction on the previously generated tokens.

- Transformer: The underlying neural network architecture (introduced in the paper "Attention Is All You Need").
- Language Model: A model trained to understand and generate human-like text.

  Common Shorthand are "Autoregressive LLM", "Causal Language Model" (since it 
uses causal masking to predict the next token) and "Transformer-based LLM".

  Note that GPT or Llama are just specific models from different companies 
(OpenAI, Meta,...) following the ATLM architecture.
