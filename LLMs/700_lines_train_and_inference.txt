## "Full stack" train+Inference in 700 lines of pure C code (no dependencies)

* <https://github.com/karpathy/llama2.c>

- (Using Llama 2 LLM architecture in PyTorch to train)

- It may be surprising but in fact, a very small LLM can have surprisingly
  strong performance for narrow domains.

- REF: TinyStories paper: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.07759>
  """ We show that TinyStories can be used to train and evaluate LMs that are 
   much smaller than the state-of-the-art models (below 10 million total 
   parameters), or have much simpler architectures (with only one transformer 
   block), yet still produce fluent and consistent stories with several paragraphs 
   that are diverse and have almost perfect grammar, and demonstrate reasoning 
   capabilities."""

- The code can also infer models trained with Llama 2, restricted to fp32.

- This project is an evolution of nanoGPT switching GPT-2 for Llama-2.

- See also <https://github.com/billxbf/nanoGPX>:<br/>
  Clean collection of modern LLM recipes (RoPE, GQA, RMSNorm, MoE, SSM, etc.) in nanoGPT style.
