## NanoChat: Build GPT from Scractch in $100

* <https://github.com/karpathy/nanochat>

- nanochat is the simplest experimental harness for training LLMs. [[training.nanochat]]
- designed to run on a single GPU node.
- the code is minimal/hackable, and it covers all major LLM stages
  including tokenization, pretraining, finetuning, evaluation, 
  inference, and a chat UI.
  - you can train your own GPT-2 capability LLM (which cost ~$43,000 to train in 2019)
    for only $48 (~2 hours of 8XH100 GPU node) and then talk to it in a familiar
    ChatGPT-like web UI. 
  - On a spot instance, the total cost can be closer to ~$15 [[PM.price.15€]]
- Configured out of the box to train an entire miniseries of compute-optimal 
  models by setting one single complexity dial:
  - `--depth`:  number of layers in the GPT transformer model (GPT-2 capability
    happens to be approximately depth 26). [[LLM.101]]
  - All other hyperparameters (width of transformer, number of heads, 
    learning rate adjustments, training horizons, weight decays, ...) are 
    calculated automatically in an optimal way.
