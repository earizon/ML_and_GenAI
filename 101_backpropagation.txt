# Backgropagation   

* Backpropagation in NN replaces the techniques of gradient descendant in simple neuronal networks with linear behaviour.

* It is one of the core building blocks of modern deep neural networks along with
  transformers, stable diffusion, ...

* Seminal paper: "Yann LeCun 1989 paper "Backpropagation Applied to Handwritten Zip Code Recognition"

* <https://github.com/karpathy/lecun1989-repro>
