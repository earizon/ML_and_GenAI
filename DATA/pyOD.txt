## PyOD: outliner detector library

* Home Page: <https://pyod.readthedocs.io/en/latest/>
* Paper: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.12154>

- Outlier detection is the identification of dataset elements that vary 
  significantly from the majority.

* comprehensive, easy-to-use  library for detecting anomalies across tabular, 
  time series, graph, text, and image data. Whether you are working with a 
  small-scale project or large datasets.

- Expanded Deep Learning Support: 12 modern neural models into a single 
  PyTorch-based framework, bringing the total number of outlier detection methods 
  to 45.

- LLM-based Model Selection: Automated model selection guided by a large 
  language model reduces manual tuning and assists users who may have limited 
  experience with outlier detection.

- Multi-Modal Detection via EmbeddingOD: Chain foundation model encoders 
  (sentence-transformers, OpenAI, HuggingFace) with any PyOD detector for
  text and image anomaly detection [ALLX+24].
