## Grassmann Flows: Attention Is Not What You Need 

Via Konsta KiirikkiKonsta

* Modern alternative to Transformers.

* <https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.19428>
"Attention Is Not What You Need: Grassmann Flows as an Attention-Free Alternative for Sequence Modeling" 

Fundamental flaw in transformer models:

- quadratic time complexity of full attention that makes long context unfeasible.

The authors suggest that attention in transformer models is just one of the
many ways of implementing tensor lifting of the token representations: self
attention computes A = softmax(QK'/sqrt(d)) in L x L for hidden states H in L x
d thus lifting them into higher dimensional representations.

The authors argue and prove in experiments that attention could be replaced
with finite dimensional flow on Grassmann manifolds! In theory this allows for
the time complexity of O(Ld^2) instead of O(L^2 d + Ld^2) for full
self-attention.

Grassmann flows open the discussion for geometric representation in sequence
modeling competing with state space models as possible future development of
sequence modeling.
